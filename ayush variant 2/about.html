About Us

GoldenPress Oils has been a trusted name in the mustard oil industry for over five decades. Our journey began in 1970 with a simple mission: to provide the purest and most nutritious mustard oil to families across the nation.

We take pride in our commitment to quality, sustainability, and community impact. Our mustard seeds are sourced from local farmers who practice sustainable agriculture, ensuring that every bottle of GoldenPress oil not only nourishes your family but also supports rural livelihoods.
